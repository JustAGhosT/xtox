# CIA 2.0 + Fluency 2.0 Canvas

## CIA 2.0 Framework (Cognitive Sovereignty)

**Core Metrics (0.0–1.0):**

* Transparency Index (TI): AI involvement clarity
* Agency Preservation Score (APS): User override/modification
* Metacognitive Awareness Rate (MAR): Reflection & verification
* Adaptive Control Range (ACR): Depth of user personalization

**Contextual Adjustments:**

* Risk-Weighted Multiplier (RW-CIA)
* Sovereignty Friction Index (SFI)
* Transparency Gap (STG)

**Leading Indicators (Tracked):**

* Sovereignty Degradation Indicators (SDI): Signs of creeping dependency
* Collective Sovereignty Impact (CSI): Team/organizational resilience

**CIA 2.0 Calculation:**
CIA2.0 = (TI + APS + MAR + ACR) / 4 × RW-CIA × SFI × (1–STG)

---

## Fluency 2.0 Framework (Sustainable Human-AI Partnership)

**Enhanced Core Metrics (0.0–1.0):**

* Onboarding Speed (OS): Time & retention
* Interaction Clarity (IC): UI clarity & mental effort
* Feedback Loops (FL): User/AI co-learning
* Confidence Transfer (CT): Calibration (confidence vs. competence)
* Error Recovery (ER): Resilience after AI errors
* Context Fluency (CF): Workflow/handoff smoothness
* Fluency Sustainability (FS): Maintenance over time

**Advanced Adjustments:**

* Fluency Trajectory Modifier (FTM)
* Workflow Integration Penalty (WIP)
* Cognitive Load Multiplier (CLM)

**Fluency 2.0 Calculation:**
Fluency2.0 = (OS + IC + FL + CT + ER + CF + FS) / 7 × FTM × WIP × CLM

**Leading Fluency Indicators (Tracked):**

* Fluency Regression Signals (FRS)
* Fluency Ceiling Indicators (FCI)
* Collective Fluency Breakdown (CFB)

## Quadrant Map

* **High CIA, High Fluency:** Sustainable excellence (top 5)
* **High CIA, Low Fluency:** Safe but hard to adopt (OSS, power-user tools)
* **Low CIA, High Fluency:** Uncommon—would be risky “fun but unsafe”
* **Low CIA, Low Fluency:** Avoid for anything but hobby use (bottom 4)

---

## Practical Recommendations

* **Top-right quadrant:** Scale for regulated, critical, or upskilling contexts
* **High CIA, Low Fluency:** Invest in training, onboarding
* **Low CIA, Low Fluency:** Avoid for enterprise/critical use
* **Monitor leading indicators:** For regression, dependency, team silos

---

## Summary

CIA 2.0 and Fluency 2.0 offer actionable, context-sensitive, sustainability-aware evaluation for human-AI collaboration—no utopia, just what can actually be measured and managed.

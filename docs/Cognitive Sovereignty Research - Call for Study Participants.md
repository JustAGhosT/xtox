## What is Cognitive Sovereignty?

As AI tools become embedded in daily work, a critical question emerges: **How do we harness AI's capabilities while preserving the human thinking that defines meaningful knowledge work?**

Our research investigates **cognitive sovereignty**‚Äîthe right to retain intentional agency and cognitive ownership in AI-mediated tasks. We're studying how different AI tools affect users' long-term cognitive capabilities, decision-making independence, and professional development.

---

## Study Objectives

### Primary Research Questions:
1. **How do different AI tools impact users' cognitive capabilities over time?**
2. **Which design features best preserve human epistemic agency while enabling productive AI collaboration?**
3. **What are the long-term effects of AI assistance on skill development and professional competence?**

### Key Measurements:
- **Cognitive Impact Assessment (CIA 2.0):** How AI systems affect reasoning independence and epistemic agency
- **Sustainable Fluency (Fluency 2.0):** Whether AI assistance creates genuine skill development or temporary performance enhancement
- **User Experience Metrics:** Satisfaction, trust calibration, and perceived cognitive ownership

## What Does Participation Involve?

### üìã Baseline Assessment (Week 1)
**Time Commitment:** 90-120 minutes
- **Cognitive Skills Assessment:** Problem-solving tasks in your domain without AI assistance
- **AI Tool Usage Survey:** Current tools, usage patterns, and preferences
- **Professional Background:** Experience, role, and work context
- **Initial Interview:** 30-45 minute discussion about AI integration in your work

### üìà Monthly Check-ins (Months 1-6)
**Time Commitment:** 30-45 minutes per month
- **Usage Tracking:** Brief survey about AI tool usage and experiences
- **Cognitive Tasks:** Short assessments measuring reasoning and problem-solving
- **Reflection Questions:** How AI assistance is affecting your work and thinking
- **Tool Evaluation:** Rating AI tools on transparency, control, and cognitive impact

### üîç Mid-Study Deep Dive (Month 3)
**Time Commitment:** 2-3 hours

- **Cognitive Ownership Analysis:** Detailed exploration of authorship and agency in AI-mediated work

### üìä Final Assessment (Month 6-12)
**Time Commitment:** 2-3 hours
- **Longitudinal Comparison:** Repeat baseline assessments to measure changes
- **Comprehensive Interview:** Detailed discussion of cognitive impacts over study period

- **Future Participation:** Optional continued involvement in follow-up studies
